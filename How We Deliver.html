How We Deliver

HR Pundits has a very strong, culturally and geographically diverse employee base, spread in the US and in India. Our teams of over 100 SAP HCM professionals specializing in various functional, technical and Project Management areas strive hard to deliver value to customers.

We have successfully delivered many implementations using a combination of Onsite/Offsite and Offshore models. This proven and time tested global delivery model enabled us to provide our customers with 24x7 support for Business critical applications such as Payroll & Time Management.

Our Project Managers are highly experienced HR professionals with years of experience in implementing SAP HCM solutions. They work with you to identify, allocate and backfill all project resources upfront. While we are doing this, we also engage with customers in training their key project resources before their project begins.
