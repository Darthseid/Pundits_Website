Our Methodology

Our Project teams are experts at developing detailed road maps blue prints for the project with specific milestones. The blue print contains clearly identified processes, and detailed steps, HCM Screen and Data fields that will be used, as well as specifications for interfaces and conversions. The more detailed the upfront planning, the better integrated design you will have, with less rework later. In this approach, customers will know exactly what to expect and can affirm the usefulness of the design before proceeding to implementation.

As an integral part of our Project implementation methodology, a great deal of importance is given to all phases of testing i.e. unit testing, system testing, acceptance testing and parallel testing. We spend a considerable amount of time on Payroll testing to ensure that there are no bugs before the system goes live. HR Pundits offers flexible engagement models for post production support services.
