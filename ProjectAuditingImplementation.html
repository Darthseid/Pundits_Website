Project Auditing & Implementation

By analyzing number of implementations, we are of the view that many of these need improvements. We also noticed that a lot of clients have been investing in continuous maintenance and support. We specialize in providing the best and stable solution with minimal post-production support. An SAP implementation is successful if the following are taken in to consideration.

  • a micro-managing project manager.

  • qualified and experienced consultants.

  • complete understanding of business needs and visualizing possible system design during business blueprint phase itself.

  • building the test-cases covering entire business scenarios.

  • achieving the milestones.

  • foreseeing the amount of effort needed for data load, data conversion and testing.

  • Making sure that the design is as per the business requirements.


As part of our project auditing, we review the solution being built and compare it with the business requirements. We also evaluate the quality of the solution being provided by the implementing company. We will also foresee any potential threats for the project time-lines and budget. We specialize in projects auditing and save money, time, efficiency and efforts.

It is common that SAP implementations cost millions of dollars and big implementation partners are awarded the contracts and the implementation partner is expected to take full responsibility and deliver a stable and complete solution within the time-lines agreed and budget.

 

It always important that an external neutral auditing is performed.
