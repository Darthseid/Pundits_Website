Enhancements & Upgrades

Enhancements: Lot of customers need few extra functionalities to accommodate new business needs and to utilize all standard functionalities delivered by SAP. Whenever additional functionality needs to be implemented, we will fully review the existing design and provide enhancement solution. As part if this enhancement, we will also evaluate the possible improvements that can be provided on the current functionality. We will put special emphasis to make sure the current processes are not affected during this enhancement process.

We will also provide the tasks to be executed at the end of the year or at the beginning of the new year. We also support the clients during “Support Pack Updates”. As part of this, we work with the clients in testing, analyzing results and resolving issues.

Upgrades: We were involved in a number of technical and functional upgrades comprising of different SAP releases. We provide a complete solution for any upgrade project. During upgrade process we review the current scope, identify the differences with the new release, build the necessary test-cases and resolve the issues for an error-free upgraded solution. We will review the current process flows, documentation and update as needed and provide additional training in the new functionality.
