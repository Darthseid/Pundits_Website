Education & Training

Our training division has trained around 700 plus consultants in US and Canadian markets. We always view that proper training is needed for the users to use the system effectively and efficiently.

Corporate Training:

it is always important that all SAP users are knowledgeable in executing SAP easy access transactions and should be able to analyze the potential issues. We focus on providing training to the corporations in the areas of their need. We provide end-user training as well as configuration training. This will enable the companies to become less dependent on external consultants thus saving lot of money. Depending on the client needs we can provide a complete training within a HCM sub-module or covering some areas only. We provide functional and technical training, including security administratiton.

Individual Consultant Training

We rate ourselves as No. 1 in providing SAP HCM training in US. Our rigirous, detailed, practical scenario-based training helped hundreds of candidates to become SAP HCM consultants. Our training runs around the year with new batches beginning once in every two weeks.
