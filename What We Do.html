What We Do

We Deliver SAP Human Capital and Talent Management Solutions

HR Technology is the engine for delivering programs and services efficiently in an organization. And while all organizations employ some aspect of technology to support their HR goals, with varying degrees of success, the right technology, delivered in the right way for right reasons, can provide services and programs seamlessly, enhance employee productivity and optimize an organization’s investment in Technology.

HR Pundits is formed by a highly experienced group of HR professionals specializing in delivering SAP HCM solutions. The company is guided under the leadership Krishna K Reddy, CEO. Mr. Reddy has over 22 years of rich HR domain experience out of which 11 years as SAP consultant in Architecting and Implementing SAP HCM Solutions to Fortune 100 clients.

Over the years, we have successfully completed SAP HCM engagements for many global, midsize and small customers. Our SAP HCM professionals are highly experienced in providing end-to-end solutions from development of business case to requirements gathering, Blue Print creation, Functional & Technical Design, code development, Testing, implementation and support. Our highly experienced Project Managers in consultation with customers assemble the right project team to fit the needs. All our consultants have a deep understanding of industry best implementation practices and routinely strike a balance between using too much of process to no process to deliver solutions cost effectively.

Implementing any SAP HCM solution requires great understanding of how to balance between Technical expertise to make the systems work efficiently and Functional HR expertise to ensure the technology solution matches the HR strategy and business goals of the Organization.

This expertise and understanding is what we bring to you as a solution partner.
